Raymond Ray | #3

FW | 6'2 | 225 lb | Age: 26

Train Insane or Remain the Same

Player Bio | To some the meaning of soccer is just a game but to Raymond, soccer is his life. Raymond first began playing soccer as soon as he could kick a soccer ball. At the age of 18, Raymond had reached his dream of joining the Manchester United, England's legendary soccer team. By the age of 20, Raymond had won 2 tournaments and signed a contract worth 2.3 million.


Early Years


California, 1992, Raymond was born into a family with a passion for atheletism and strive to succeed. At an early age Raymond strived to be the best on his school soccer team and on through university showed to be a promising football player. Winning tournament after tournament Raymond's hard work and dedication had not gone unnoticed. It wasn't long until the Manchester United Club had scouted him and soon signed Raymond as an offical player. By 2012, Raymond was a full-time forward.
